Dynamic Pricing Mechanism

Zero Initial Supply: XBOT will launch with a zero initial supply and a pre-determined starting price.

Price Evolution: The token price will dynamically adjust after each transaction. When you buy XBOT, the contract will calculate the number of tokens you receive based on the amount of ETH you use and the current token price. After each buy-in, the token price will slightly increase, and vice versa for selling.
